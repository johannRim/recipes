Ingredients:
 - 100g of sweet pancetta
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 1 onion, chopped
 - 2 tablespoons Extra virgin olive oil
 - 1 teaspoon pepperencinno
 - 2 table spoon of pesto
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - One medium size bowl of left over rice (the drier the better)

Directions:
 1. Brown the pancetta
 2. Add the onion, garlic till soft
 3. Add the pepperencinno
 4. Add the olive oil
 5. Add the rice, making sure its coated with the oil
 6. Add the pesto
